# Product Context

register: brand

## Product Purpose

An official webnovel and fantasy author website for The Wandering Fairy that feels personal, reader-facing, and easy to maintain. The site should introduce the author, showcase the novel, publish updates, collect newsletter signups, link to Amazon and Royal Road, and let fans check writing progress.

## Users

Readers who already know the author, new visitors discovering a series, fans checking the next-book tracker, and publishing or business contacts looking for author information.

## Brand Tone

Cozy, bookish, nostalgic, fan-community oriented, and lightly theatrical. It should feel like a beloved early 2000s fantasy personal website rebuilt by a careful modern developer.

## Strategic Principles

- Put the author at the center rather than making the site feel like a corporate publisher.
- Make recurring content data-driven so books, progress bars, art, and posts are easy to add.
- Use old-web flavor intentionally: boxed layout, decorative borders, badges, visible links, chunky headings, and progress meters.
- Keep modern basics intact: responsive layout, semantic HTML, accessible labels, clear focus states, and readable contrast.

## Anti-References

Generic SaaS landing pages, glassmorphism, neon cyberpunk, hard-to-read novelty typography, broken retro styling, and layouts that hide the author behind product marketing polish.
